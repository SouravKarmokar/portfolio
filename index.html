A bit about me

I'm a final-year Electrical & Electronic Engineering student at Daffodil International University with a genuine interest in embedded systems — particularly STM32-based development — and solar energy systems. I enjoy working close to hardware and understanding how things work at the circuit level.

A Software-Defined Digital Twin Framework for Enhanced Tele-Laboratory Manipulation in Bio-Hazardous and High-Risk Environments
A software-defined teleoperation framework for a 4-DOF lab manipulator using a 60 Hz digital twin, with predictive jitter compensation and a collision-safe envelope. Validated across 20 non-expert operators, showing reduced task completion time and safety incidents over conventional teleoperation.
